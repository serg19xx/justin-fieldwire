Schedule a call via SMS

FieldWire — PM offers three time slots; client replies 1, 2, or 3; event is created in Calendar

New Clients workflow: agree on a call time by SMS without manual back-and-forth. When the client confirms a slot, a calendar event is created automatically for the PM.

How time slots are chosen

The PM picks the date (at least 3 days from today, no one-week cap).

FieldWire shows the full day schedule in 30-minute steps (9:00 AM–5:00 PM). Busy blocks are marked unavailable (Calendar events or slots held by another pending SMS invite).

The PM selects 1 to 3 free slots for the client. After sending, they are held for 3 days until the client replies or the invite expires.

How it works

PM → FieldWire → SMS to client (3 slots)
Client → SMS reply: 1, 2, or 3
FieldWire → PM Calendar event + confirmation SMS
  • Invites expire after 72 hours.
  • A new invite cancels any previous pending invite for the same client.
  • Reply must come from the same phone that received the SMS.
  • Default timezone: America/Toronto.

Where to find it

  1. Sign in as Admin or Project Manager.
  2. Clients → choose a list.
  3. Row action Schedule call via SMS (client must have a phone).
  4. Fill date, three slots, duration → Send meeting invite.

Sample invite SMS

FieldWire — meeting request Hi Dr. Smith, Jane Doe (Project Manager) would like to schedule a call with you. Topic: Call with Dr. Smith Date: Jun 22, 2026 (30 min) Reply with your preferred time: 1) 10 AM 2) 2 PM 3) 3:30 PM Text back 1, 2, or 3.

Sample confirmation (client replies 2)

FieldWire — confirmed Thank you, Dr. Smith! Call with Dr. Smith Jun 22, 2026 at 2 PM — Jane Doe, Project Manager

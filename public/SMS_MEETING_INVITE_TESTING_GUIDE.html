Testing: Schedule a call via SMS

FieldWire · v2.0 · PM picks date and slots · 3-day hold · Calendar + Twilio

Goal: PM offers call times by SMS; client confirms by reply; event appears in PM Calendar.

1. What was delivered

  • PM picks meeting date (min +3 days, no one-week cap)
  • Day schedule grid: 30-min steps, 9 AM–5 PM; busy blocks marked
  • PM selects 1–3 free slots; held 3 days until client reply
  • Clear SMS; client reply 1 / 2 / 3; Calendar event on confirm

2. Prerequisites

  • Admin or Project Manager account
  • Client with phone on record
  • Mobile to read/reply SMS
  • Test date ≥ today + 3 days

4. Additional scenarios

  • Min date: cannot pick tomorrow; +3 days works
  • Busy date: pick another date with free slots
  • Hold: sent slots show Held for SMS invite for other clients
  • 1–2 slots: SMS says «1» or «1 or 2»
  • Invalid reply: hello → prompt; then valid digit → confirm
  • Calendar overlap: event blocks matching grid slot
  • Roles: Worker no access; PM/Admin yes
